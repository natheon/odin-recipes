Buckwheat Crepes

Description

Buckwheat crÃªpes are very thin pancakes made out of buckwheat. They can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Buckwheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Whisk the flour and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in the water.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.
